Abu Sayed

About:

A student at Begum Rokeya University in Rangpur, was a leading coordinator of the nationwide protests calling for quota reform. His death, the first of six on July 16, sparked widespread outrage across Bangladesh, transforming the quota reform movement into a broader anti-government protest.
